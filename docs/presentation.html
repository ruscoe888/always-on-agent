The Situation
Sound familiar?
  • 🔔
    Incidents land in a queue. Your team reads each one, searches for a matching runbook, and manually decides severity. This takes 20-40 minutes per incident — during which your customers are waiting.
  • 🔗
    Deploy correlations are guesswork. "Did a recent release cause this?" requires a human to cross-reference incident timestamps against deploy logs. The answer is usually obvious in hindsight — and late.
  • 📄
    Compliance drift goes unnoticed. Vendor contracts are signed and filed. No one has time to re-audit them against your policy each quarter. Violations accumulate silently until an incident forces a review.
  • 🌙
    Nights and weekends are unmonitored. Issues that surface at 2am sit untriaged until the morning standup. By then, enterprise customers have already called your CEO.
